Biography

Mr. Miao Yin, from Chengdu, China, now is a PhD student in Computer Engineering (advised by Prof. Bo Yuan) at Rutgers University. Before that, he was an algorithm engineer at SOHU.com inc. Mr. Miao Yin reaped his Master's degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering (supervised by Prof. Guangmin Hu and Prof. Feng Qian) from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2018. Previously, he received his Bachelor's degree in Communication Engineering from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2013.

Mr. Miao Yin's research interests are focused in Tensor, Deep Learning and Neural Network Compression.
