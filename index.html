Biography

Mr. Miao Yin, from Chengdu, China, now is a PhD candidate of Computer Engineering (advised by Prof. Bo Yuan) at Rutgers University. Before that, he was an algorithm engineer at SOHU.com inc. Mr. Miao Yin reaped his Master's degree of Electronics and Communication Engineering (advised by Prof. Guangmin Hu and Prof. Feng Qian) from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2018. Previously, he received his Bachelor's degree of Communication Engineering from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2013.

Mr. Miao Yin's research interests are focused in Tensor, Deep Learning and Neural Network Compression.
